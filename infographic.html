📥 4 Ways to Log Meals
📸

Food Photo

Snap a picture of your meal and post it. GPT-4o Vision identifies every food item, estimates portions, and calculates macros + calories automatically.

📦

Barcode Scan

Photograph a product barcode. The bot decodes it, looks up Open Food Facts for exact nutrition data. Add quantity modifiers like half, 2 servings, or 50g.

✍️

Text Description

Just type what you ate: "two scrambled eggs, toast with butter, and a coffee". Claude AI estimates the macros and kcal from your description.

🎤

Voice Message

Record a voice note describing your meal. OpenAI Whisper transcribes it, then Claude analyzes the text for full nutrition breakdown.

✏️ Correction Workflow
1
Bot posts a 📊 Nutrition Breakdown embed after analyzing your meal
2
Reply to that embed with text or voice: "the steak is 200g not 300g, the radish are tomatoes"
3
Bot detects it's a correction → sends original analysis + your changes to Claude AI
4
Claude re-evaluates the entire meal with corrections applied → updates the database
5
Bot replies with a ✏️ Corrected Nutrition Breakdown (orange embed) + updated daily budget

🎯 Smart Budget Tracking
📊

Auto Budget After Each Meal

Every time you log a meal, the bot instantly shows your remaining kcal, divided evenly across your remaining meal windows for the day.

🔥

Body Fat Calculation

At end of day, calculates how much body fat you burned (or gained) based on your deficit/surplus. Uses the 7,700 kcal = 1kg fat formula.

⏰

6 Meal Windows

Day runs 4am → 3:59am. Six windows: Breakfast (8–11), Morning Snack (11–14), Lunch (14–17), Afternoon Snack (17–20), Dinner (20–23), Evening (23–4).

⚙️ Tech Stack
🐍

Python + discord.py

Bot framework with privileged intents for message content and member events.

🧠

Claude AI (Anthropic)

Text meal analysis, quantity interpretation, and correction re-evaluation.

👁️

GPT-4o Vision (OpenAI)

Food photo analysis — identifies items, estimates portions, calculates macros.

🗣️

Whisper (OpenAI)

Voice message transcription for hands-free meal logging.

📦

Open Food Facts

Free barcode database for exact product nutrition (no API key needed).

🚂

Railway + SQLite

Hosted on Railway with persistent SQLite volume for meal & user data.
