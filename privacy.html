Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FoodTracker ("the Bot", "we", "our"), operated by Rozek Industries Ltd., collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Discord bot.

The short version: We store your meal data (calories, macros, descriptions) so the bot works. We don't sell your data. Food photos and voice messages are processed by AI APIs and not permanently stored by us.

1. Information We Collect

Discord User Data: Your Discord user ID and display name. We do not collect your email, password, or other Discord account details.

Meal Data: When you log a meal, we store: the date and time, calorie and macro estimates (protein, carbs, fat), a text description of the meal, and which meal window it belongs to. This data is stored in our database to provide tracking, budgets, and leaderboards.

User Preferences: Your calorie target, premium/trial status, and interaction counts.

Content You Submit: Food photos, voice messages, and text descriptions are sent to third-party AI services for analysis (see Section 3). We do not permanently store photos or audio files on our servers — they are processed in real-time and discarded.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to provide FoodTracker's features: meal analysis, calorie budget tracking, daily summaries, leaderboard rankings, and reminders. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the Bot's functionality.

3. Third-Party AI Services

To analyze your meals, we send data to the following third-party AI APIs:

Google Gemini API (primary) — Processes food photos, text descriptions, voice messages, and barcode quantity interpretation. Subject to Google's API Terms of Service.

OpenAI API (fallback) — May process food photos (GPT-4o Vision) and voice messages (Whisper). Subject to OpenAI's Terms of Use.

Anthropic API (fallback) — May process text meal descriptions. Subject to Anthropic's Terms of Service.

These services process your content to generate nutritional estimates. We do not control how these providers handle data — please review their respective privacy policies. API providers' data retention policies apply to content sent through their APIs.

Open Food Facts — Barcode lookups are sent to the Open Food Facts API (a public, open-source food database). No personal data is included in barcode requests.

4. Data Storage and Security

Meal records and user preferences are stored in a SQLite database on our hosting provider (Railway). The database is stored on a persistent volume with access restricted to the Bot's runtime environment.

We take reasonable measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security. No internet-based service is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

Your meal data is retained as long as your account is active in FoodTracker. If you wish to have your data deleted, contact us (see Section 9) and we will remove all records associated with your Discord user ID within 30 days.

Photos and voice messages are not stored — they are processed in memory and immediately discarded after analysis.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except as described in Section 3 (AI processing) and as required by law.

Within FoodTracker, the following data is shared with other users in your Discord server: meal summaries (food description and calorie total) posted to the group channel, and leaderboard rankings (display name and calorie adherence). Your detailed macro breakdowns and photos remain in your private channel.

7. Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Discord through their Premium App Subscriptions system. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information (credit card, billing address, etc.). For payment-related concerns, refer to Discord's Privacy Policy.

8. Children's Privacy

FoodTracker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 is using FoodTracker, please contact us and we will delete their data.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to: request a copy of the data we store about you, request deletion of all your data, stop using the Bot at any time (your data will remain unless you request deletion). To exercise these rights, contact: rob@robrhose.com

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact: rob@robrhose.com
