Made a Mistake? Just Reply

If the bot gets something wrong, reply to the nutrition breakdown with your corrections — by text or voice message. The bot re-evaluates the entire meal.

Example replies
🗣️ "the steak is 200g not 300g"
🗣️ "those aren't radishes, they're tomatoes"
🗣️ "remove the bread, I didn't eat it"
🗣️ "add a glass of orange juice"
💡

You can also use !undo to remove the last meal, !delete 2 to remove a specific meal, or !edit 2 kcal=400 to manually adjust values.
